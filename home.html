Welcome to NaijaHerbivore Heaven!!!

At NaijaHerbivoreH, we focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients while staying true to the bold flavors of Nigerian cuisine. Our farm-to-table approach ensures that every dish we serve supports local farmers, celebrates community, and embodies the spirit of sustainability.

Our Farm-to-Table Approach

1. Partnering with Local Farms: We work with Houston-area farms and urban gardens to source fresh vegetables, greens, and herbs, ensuring dishes like efo riro (spinach stew) and yam porridge are made with the freshest ingredients.

2. Emphasizing Traditional Ingredients: For spices and ingredients not locally available, we collaborate with African importers in Houston to maintain authentic flavors while supporting local businesses.

3. Building Community Partnerships: By teaming up with local organizations focused on food sustainability, we promote food sovereignty and seasonal dishes that inspire the community.

4. Creative Vegetarian Dishes: From vegetarian jollof rice to moi moi (steamed bean pudding), we combine bold Nigerian flavors with a modern, plant-based twist.
