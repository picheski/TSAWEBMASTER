Our History!

In 2023, as the world began to recover from the challenges of quarantine, NaijaHerbivoreHeaven opened its doors with a mission to celebrate Nigerian culture and create a vibrant space for community connection. Rooted in the rich traditions of Nigerian cuisine, the restaurant combines bold, authentic flavors with a commitment to sustainability and inclusivity. By sourcing fresh, local ingredients and offering a welcoming environment, NaijaHerbivoreHeaven has become a hub for sharing stories, building relationships, and embracing the essence of Nigerian heritage. This space not only provides exceptional dining experiences but also fosters a sense of belonging, unity, and pride for all who enter.

Food Preparation Process

Proper food preparation is critical to maintaining health and safety. Our guidelines ensure that food is handled with care:

  • Avoid Cross Contamination: Keep raw and cooked foods separate.
  • Color Code Thawing: Clearly designate areas and tools for different ingredients.
  • Wash Hands Constantly: Maintain hygiene during every step of preparation.
  • Monitor Food Temperature: Ensure food is cooked and stored at safe temperatures.

Our Approach to Sustainability

Reduce Energy Waste

  • Use timers and motion detector lights.
  • Install low-energy LED lighting.
  • Turn off POS systems after night shifts.
  • Service appliances regularly to ensure efficiency.

Reduce Physical Waste

  • Donate leftover food to nonprofits.
  • Join local recycling programs.
  • Use smart inventory management systems to reduce over-ordering.

Eliminate Single-Use Plastics

  • Replace plastic takeout containers with recyclable alternatives.
  • Allow customers to opt out of receiving plastic cutlery and straws.
  • Switch to bamboo or cardboard straws.
  • Invest in sustainable products with eco-friendly packaging.
