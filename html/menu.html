Apps

  • Peppered Vegetable Suya - Grilled skewers of marinated vegetables, spiced with traditional Nigerian suya seasoning.
  • Akara (Spicy Bean Cakes) - Crispy fritters made from black-eyed peas, seasoned with herbs and spices, served with a tangy dipping sauce.
  • Plantain Chips - Thinly sliced and fried plantains, lightly salted and served with a zesty avocado dip.
  • Moin Moin - Spiced steamed bean pudding with the option of a boiled egg inside.

Mains

  • Egusi Soup - Rich melon seed soup cooked with spinach and spices, served with a side of fufu made from cassava.
  • Efo Riro - Nigerian spiced spinach stew served with fufu.
  • Jollof Rice - Flavorful rice cooked in a spicy tomato sauce, accompanied by seasonal vegetables and plant-based proteins.
  • Vegetable Banga Rice - Fragrant rice cooked in palm nut sauce with assorted vegetables and spices, a hearty satisfying dish.
  • Yam and "Egg" - Eggs or Tofu scrambled with tomatoes, peppers, and onions with the choice of either boiled or fried yam.
  • Beans and Plantain - Beans cooked in a thick spicy broth topped with sweet ripe plantains.

Sweet Endings

  • Coconut Rice Pudding - Creamy rice pudding infused with coconut milk and topped with fresh mango slices.
  • Nutty Chocolate Truffles - Peanut butter and almond balls covered in melted dark chocolate and coated with coconut shavings.
  • Chin Chin - Crunchy, sweet fried dough snacks, lightly spiced and perfect for sharing.
  • Fruit Salad Medley
  • Puff Puff - Light fried dough balls with the following choice of toppings: caramel sauce, chocolate sauce, or powdered sugar.
